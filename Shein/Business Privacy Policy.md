4.Children

Our services are not directed to, and we do not knowingly collect Personal Information from, children under the age of 16 or minors (as defined by applicable national laws). If a minor has provided us with Personal Information without parental or guardian consent, the parent or guardian should contact us immediately to remove the relevant Personal Information and de-register the minor.

If we become aware that a minor has provided us with Personal Information, we will take steps to promptly delete such information from our files. As described further in our Agreement, please do not upload photographs showing other people, in particular if they are minors.

5.Sharing Your Personal Information

We may disclose and share your Personal Information with the parties as described below:

5.1 Internal SHEIN Sharing. SHEIN is part of a broader group of companies collectively referred to as the “SHEIN Group”. SHEIN may share your Personal Information with other members of the SHEIN Group to conclude or fulfill contracts and other legitimate interests to fulfill lawful contracts.

5.2 Legal and Regulatory Compliance. SHEIN may share or disclose Personal Information to meet or comply with its legal and regulatory obligations, and to comply with valid, enforceable legal requests.

5.3 Consent. SHEIN may share or disclose Personal Information collected from you for other reasons not set out in this Privacy Notice with your separate consent.

5.4 Service Providers. SHEIN may share your Personal Information collected with third party services providers who provide logistics services, fraud prevention and risk control services or legal compliance services. We strive to ensure that our third-party service providers can only use the Personal Information collected from Sellers for the purposes for which it was provided to them and to the extent necessary for such purposes, in accordance with our written instructions.

5.5 Payment Service Providers. SHEIN may share your Personal Information with third-party payment services providers who provide payment processing and remittance services to you in accordance with the agreement between you and them. Independently from SHEIN, the third-party payment services providers are the controllers of such Personal Information and responsible for any processing that they perform after we have shared such Personal Information with them in accordance with the applicable laws and their privacy policy.

5.6 Buyers on the Marketplace. Where required by applicable laws or regulations, SHEIN may share Seller’s information including name, business address, business email address with buyers on the Marketplace for compliance with the legal obligations.

5.7 Corporate Transactions. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or reorganization, SHEIN may share Personal Information collected from Sellers with the acquiring or surviving entity. We will notify the relevant individuals of the name and contact information of the recipient of the transferred Personal Information.

*   Withdrawal of Consent. You have the right to withdraw your consent to processing under this Privacy Notice by contacting SHEIN. Withdrawal of consent may limit your ability to use the Services.
*   Data Deletion Requests. If you withdraw your consent to processing under this Privacy Notice by providing written notice to us, you may request deletion of your Personal Information.
